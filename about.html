The Atlas gym was created in April of 2023 with the hope to help others accomplish their goals no matter what others say. After falling off a roof and breaking his arm, Jacob met with one of his physical therapists who said that he would never be able to lift weights again. Today he enjoys going to the gym six days a week and is stronger then he was before he broke his arm. He hopes that from this experience he can help others understand that their hopes and dreams are possible with belief, conmmitment, and consistency.

Atlas is a multilevel gym with equipment and opportunity for all who want to join. Atlas includes a weight room, a swimming pool, a running track, and rooms for you to indivisually work with our personal trainers.

We welcome all to join us and try out our free one week trial. If you are interested in joining, feel free to contact us at (702)322-8527!
